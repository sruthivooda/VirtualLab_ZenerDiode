ZENER DIODE

Procedure

  1. Connect 1K resistor with Emitter of the Transistor

  2. Connect 330R resistor with Collector of the Transistor

  3. Connect LED +ve with the 1K resistor

  4. Connect Zener diode anode(+) to the base of the Transistor

  5. Connect remaining terminal of diode and the 330R resistor and connect it to the +ve terminal of the battery.

  6. Connect the other end of LED to the -ve terminal of the battery.
